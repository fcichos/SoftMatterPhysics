    "Diffusion:\n",
    "\n",
    "$N(x)$ on the left \n",
    "$N(x+\\Delta x)$ on the right of area $A$\n",
    "\n",
    "\n",
    "$$\n",
    "j=-\\frac{1}{2}\\frac{N(x+\\Delta x)-N(x))}{A\\tau}\n",
    "$$\n",
    "\n",
    "\n",
    "density\n",
    "\n",
    "$$\n",
    "n(x)\\equiv \\frac{N(x)}{A\\Delta x}\n",
    "$$\n",
    "\n",
    "$$\n",
    "j=-\\frac{1}{2}\\frac{n(x+\\Delta x)A\\Delta x-N(x)A\\Delta x)}{A\\tau}\n",
    "$$\n",
    "\n",
    "$$\n",
    "j=-\\frac{1}{2}\\frac{\\Delta x^2}{\\tau}\\frac{n(x+\\Delta x)-n(x)}{\\Delta x}\n",
    "$$\n",
    "\n",
    "$$\n",
    "j=-D\\frac{dn}{dx}\n",
    "$$\n",
    "\n",
    "with \n",
    "\n",
    "$$\n",
    "D=\\frac{\\Delta x^2}{2\\tau}\n",
    "$$\n",
    "or\n",
    "\n",
    "$$\n",
    "\\textbf{j}=-D\\nabla n \\tag{1. Ficks Law}\n",
    "$$\n",
    "\n",
    "combine with continuity eq. \n",
    "\n",
    "$$\n",
    "\\frac{dn}{dt}=D\\nabla^2n \\tag{2. Ficks Law}\n",
    "$$\n",
    "\n",
    "general diffusion equation. $n$ exchangable by other densities:\n",
    "\n",
    "| flux | transport property | gradient |\n",
    "| :---: | :---: | :---: |\n",
    "| particles | diffusivity | particle density |\n",
    "| charge | conductivity | potential |\n",
    "| liquid | permeability | pressure |\n",
    "| momentum | viscosity | momentum density |\n",
    "| energy | heat conductivity | temperature |"
